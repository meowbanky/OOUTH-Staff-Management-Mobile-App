Privacy Policy for OOUTH Mobile

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

OOUTH Mobile ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our HR & Salary Management mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information:

  • Employee login credentials (email, password)
  • Employee identification information (name, staff ID, department)
  • Salary and payroll information
  • Biometric authentication data (fingerprint/face recognition)

2.2 Device Information:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage analytics
  • Crash reports and performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Uses:

  • Provide HR and salary management services
  • Authenticate user access to the application
  • Display salary information and payslips
  • Send notifications and updates

4. Data Storage and Security

Security Measures:
  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Biometric data is stored locally on your device
  • Login credentials are encrypted and stored securely
  • Regular security audits are conducted

5. Data Sharing

We Do Not Share:

  • Personal salary information
  • Employee identification details
  • Biometric data
  • Login credentials

6. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase: Authentication and analytics
  • OneSignal: Push notification services

7. Your Rights

  • View your personal information stored in the app
  • Update your profile information
  • Request deletion of your account data
  • Opt-out of non-essential notifications

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
